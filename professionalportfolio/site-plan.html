Content

Home page

Home Page will be visited by people to see a summary about me (name, short description, and much others information), my projects, skills, contact info, feedback, metrics that support my knowledge, experiences and achievements. Plus, They will come in for a quick checkup of a potential employee to your business. This page is to show who i am as a Professional Employee and what technologies i use. Visitors will have my contact information or link of contact page accessible.

Images for the Home page

Contact

Contact page will mainly be visited by people after visiting the home page, to obtain my contact information, such as telephone number, residence address, country, full name and email. Visitors will be able to click a button linked with my phone number to open the calling application from a smartphone with the already copied phone number ready to call and a whatsApp button linked to the whatsApp web application using my phone number to start a chat.

Images for the Contact page

Project Gallery

Project Gallery page will mainly be visited by people after visiting the home page to look for images that support my experiences, satisfied clients and skills. Also, visitors will be able to read a short description about the project and sometimes click over a button to open a new tab with the link of a developed application or web page. This pages is for support the main idea about having the best chances to hiring me as a new employee.
